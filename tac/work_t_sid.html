Singapore Institute of Directors

We built a bespoke Content Management Solution for Singapore Institute of Directors to control their pages including; updating annual publication and articles, events management complete with registration and payment gateway, as well as capabilities to create polls or surveys and publishing it.

The Customer Relation Manager (CRM) enables administrator to view and manage members’ details, subscription status, activities log, and payment history. At the same time, to continually update their members, we’ve built an emailer engine for them to send out newsletter.

Project Completed: November 2009

Industry: Non-profit
