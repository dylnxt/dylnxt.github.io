Mead Johnson Enfawidget

The brief was to create brand awareness for "Enfa", motherhood products from Mead Johnson. A desktop widget was proposed as part of the brand awareness campaign.

We were involved in the development the particular widget, and after studying various platform for widget, we chose Adobe Flex as our tool. The widget was built with expecting mothers needs in mind, it is capable of keeping track pregnancy progress, provides suggested diets, nutrition, and exercises. The widget also dynamically updates information from a Content Management System built separately.

Project Completed: April 2010

Industry: Food and Beverages
