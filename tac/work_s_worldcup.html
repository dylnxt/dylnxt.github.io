Novena World Cup Prediction

Novena World Cup Prediction is the latest campaign done by Novena to enhance their branding and focus as a sports mall.

The application is developed as a facebook contest app for users to predict world cup scores before the actual match starts, and to gain the highest points by guessing the correct match outcome. It took us around 6 days of development starting from the brainstorming, design and execution
