SAP Flexidesk

FlexiDesk is a flexible online booking for physical office workspaces in SAP, impliedly, the service is only available for SAP staff and we can only divulge a few tantalizing details.

It is flexible because FlexiDesk provides access to workspaces in numerous buildings spanning across the globe on different timezones. The system also scalable to add more floorplans in the future. If this is not exciting enough, FlexiDesk also records your past bookings and provide you with a quick preferred booking if you need to reserve again.

We wish we could show you more, but we're such a tease!
