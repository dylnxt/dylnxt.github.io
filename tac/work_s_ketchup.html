McDonald’s - Ketchup

Ketchup is a social networking website available exclusively for McDonald's staffs. Users can arrange internal events, to chat and discuss, and ultimately increase the relationship between staffs, and try to achieve workplace satisfaction. It is also a medium for corporate to share human resource improvement materials, best practices, and events.

The portal was built with similar capabilities to Facebook including profile management, photo galleries, private mails, and groups

Project Completed: January 2010

Industry: Food and Beverages

The Works: Networking Platform / Campaign Management System
