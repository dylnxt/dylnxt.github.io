Our Story

She fell for him because he was shy with cute dimples and bathed twice a day. He, on the other hand, was attracted to Krystle because she was smart, unpretentious and (fortunately) shorter than he was.

One and a half years of stolen glances, protracted MSN conversations and prayers later, we became a couple.

Now, close to five years on, we are finally getting married. We firmly believe that leaving and cleaving in God’s design is one of the greatest gifts ever. And commitment, far from being a dirty word, is the threshold requirement to a lifelong adventure.

Join us on the most important day of our lives as we vow to love and honour each other for as long as we live.
